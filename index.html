About Garfield

Garfield is a sarcastic orange cat who lives with his owner Jon and a dog named Odi. Garfield loves to eat. His favorite food is lasagna, and he also likes pizza and donuts.

Characteristics

  • Easy Going
  • Lazy
  • Sarcastic
  • Selfish
  • Funny
