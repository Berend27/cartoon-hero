About Garfield

Garfield is a sarcastic orange cat who lives with his owner Jon and a dog named Odi. Garfield loves to eat. His favorite food is lasagna.

Characteristics

  1. Funny
  2. Lazy
  3. Easy Going
  4. Sarcastic
  5. Selfish
